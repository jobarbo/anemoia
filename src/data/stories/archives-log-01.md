I am the Archivist. That is at least what people used to call me, back when we had to hide just to exist. By exist, I mean read, seek knowledge, meet, exchange, or indulge in any leisure deemed forbidden by Trudeyl Corp. But today, there is no one left around me. I am alone. I have but one purpose: to document the years of horror that my fellow citizens and I have lived through. Nothing more. So that such an era of terror may never come again.
