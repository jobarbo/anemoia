I am the Archivist. That is at least what people called me once, in a time when we had to hide simply to exist. By exist, I mean read, seek information, gather, exchange, or pursue any leisure activity deemed forbidden by T-CORP Corp. But today, there is no one left around me. I am alone. I have but one will: to document the years of horror that my fellow citizens and I lived through. Nothing more. So that such an era of terror may never come again.
